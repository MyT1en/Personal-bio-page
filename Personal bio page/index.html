    Hello, I'm Tien Nguyen! I am a graphic design student currently in my senior year. This spring, I designed a typeface that took inspiration from Vietnamese Calligraphy. It's one of my proudest works. Vietnamese calligraphy is considered to be a great form of self expression and art. As an Asian American, I grew up seeing Vietnamese Calligraphy during the lunar new years.

    My mom just recently came back from her trip to Vietnam with gifts. She knew that I was getting into calligraphy, so she bought me the proper ink, paper, and brushes to learn. She knows how interested I am in it. I plan to further refine my craft, and create posters and shirts with it.
